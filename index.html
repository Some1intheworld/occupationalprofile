Video Game Designer

Design and improve game mechanics and game play

Daily Duties

  • Designs game mechanics, features, rules, storylines, guides, maps, environment, challenges, characters, etc.
  • Improve existing games, come up with new ideas, balance and adjust game play, oversee game play testing, and ensure the success of the game.
  • Communicate and talk to clients to ensure their expectations are met
  • Following video games industry standards and best practices
  • Prototyping new games
  • Game testing
  • Conducting quality control

Work in an office setting in a creative group environment Approximately 40-60 hours/week

$60k - $150k

They also need to review current literature, talk with colleagues, participate in educational programs, attend meetings or workshops, or participate in professional organizations or conferences to keep the game up to industry standards
