Barriers

Barriers that might limit my ability to succeed or become a game designer

Financial

Getting a degree from a university/college related to this field is very expensive. Getting a bachelor of information technology at Ontario Tech University costs up to $9000 for domestic students, excluding the ancillary fees.

How to overcome?

  • Apply to OSAP for financial assistance
  • Working part-time to earn and save some money
  • Ask parents for financial aid

Finding a Job

Game design is a highly competitive field, and junior designers could struggle to find a job without prior experience. Building a strong portfolio is also crucial. Finding a job in big companies and earning a high income can be very challenging for a junior designer as well

How to overcome?

  • Start early and gain experience
  • Connect and get to know people in this field of work to gain insight and tips
  • Start small, work for a small company or clients first
  • Make small and big projects along the way to build up the portfolio

  • Practice time management and focus skills early to maximize productivity in the future and reduce work hours
